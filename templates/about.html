About Us

What is AMPKen?

The Association of Medical Physics of Kenya promotes physics in medicine, improves healthcare quality, and supports the professional development of medical physicists across Kenya.

Our Mission

Advancing medical physics in Kenya

To advance the field of medical physics in Kenya through education, research, and professional development — ensuring the highest standards in the application of physics to medicine.

Our Vision

A leading voice in medical physics

To be a leading organisation in medical physics, recognised for excellence, innovation and professional integrity at the national and regional level.

Our Values

What we stand for

Excellence
Innovation
Integrity
Collaboration
Professionalism
Our Objectives

What we aim to achieve

1
Promote the application of physics in medicine and biology
2
Encourage research and development in medical physics
3
Facilitate continuing education and professional development
4
Foster collaboration between medical physicists and other healthcare professionals
5
Advocate for the highest standards of patient care and radiation safety
